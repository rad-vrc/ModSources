受够了背包被各种各样的宠物/坐骑/时装/抓钩填满？这个mod让它们全部可堆叠！

这个mod应该能极大优化东方小伙伴/灾厄虚荣等宠物时装mod的游戏体验，对需要大量刷boss的mod体验也有一定优化

这些物品无法获得词缀，所以它们本就应该可堆叠

更新1.1：一些工具现在也可以堆叠，例如钓竿，魔镜，生命木魔棒等

不用担心词缀问题，mod会对物品能否获得词缀进行检测，所以如果你安装了其他给这些物品增加词缀的mod，这些物品就不再可堆叠

更新1.2：现在可以一键装备了！单个物品右键为装备，多个物品堆叠右键为拿取一个！

“Tired of your inventory being filled with all sorts of pets, mounts, vanity items, and grapple hooks? This mod makes them all stackable!

This mod should greatly improve the gameplay experience for pet-and-vanity-item–heavy mods like Touhou Pets and Calamity Vanity, and it also offers optimizations for mods that require extensive boss killing.”

These items cannot receive prefixes, so they should inherently be stackable.

Update 1.1: Some tools can now be stacked, such as fishing rods, magic mirrors, living wood wands, etc

Don't worry about prefixes, mods will detect whether items can obtain prefixes, so if you install other mods that add prefixes to these items, they will no longer be stackable

Update 1.2: Now you can equip them with one click! Right-click single items to equip, multiple stacked items to take one!
