Allows you to move items between your inventory and chests with a simple click and drag. 
No need to click on every slot in the inventory, just simply hold down the mouse button and let the mod do the rest. 

[Additional Features]
- [c/00FF00:Splittable Grab Bags]: Allows grab bags to be split with Shift + Right Click 
- [c/00FF00:Drag Throwing]: Allows items to be thrown while dragging and holding the throw key

[Config Options]
- [c/FFFF00:Left Mouse.Enabled]: Enables dragging when the left mouse button is held
- [c/FFFF00:Right Mouse.Enabled]: Enables dragging when the right mouse button is held
- [c/FFFF00:Allow Ctrl]: Allows dragging when Ctrl is held
- [c/FFFF00:Allow Shift]: Allows dragging when Shift is held
- [c/FFFF00:Allow Alt]: Allows dragging when Alt is held
- [c/FFFF00:Allow Throw]: Allows dragging when the Throw key is held
- [c/FFFF00:Require Modifier]: Only allows dragging if a modifier (Ctrl, Shift, Alt, Throw) is being held
- [c/FFFF00:Splittable Grab Bags.Enabled]: Enables if grab bags can be split with Shift + Right Click 
- [c/FFFF00:Splittable Grab Bags.Show Tooltip]: Whether or not to show the controls on the toolip
